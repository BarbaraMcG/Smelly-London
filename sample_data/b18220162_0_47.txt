40 Of all the London Boroughs, only four have over 100 per 1,000 male workers engaged in metal work, viz:—Woolwich (157), Greenwich (124), Deptford (123), and Poplar (109). As regards "Unoccupied" and "Retired," only five Boroughs have fewer than Deptford, viz:—Woolwich (118), Bermondsey (116), Hammersmith (115), Southwark (111), and the City of London (95). The Registrar General gives a nil return under the following occupations for males in Deptford:—Fishermen, mining and quarry, makers of tobacco, cigars, cigarettes and snuff, makers of paper, pulp, straw and cardboard.
