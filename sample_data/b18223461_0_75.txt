No case of Encephalitis Lethargica was notified during the year, four visits to old cases of Encephalitis Lethargica were made by the Woman Sanitary Inspector. Encephalitis Lethargica is undoubtedly infectious, but the infectivity is of very low nature. The sequelæ may be serious, as in most cases some permanent damage to the brain tissue occurs. The disease was made compulsorily notifiable from 1st January, 1919. Deaths.—There were two deaths _ from this disease during the year. CEREBRO-SPINAL FEVER.
