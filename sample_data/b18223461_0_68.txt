Measles. Food Poisoning. Whooping Cough. NOTIFICATIONS DURING THE YEAR. The total number of notifications, excluding duplicates, was 343. Of this figure, 49 were notifications of Pulmonary Tuberculosis and 5 of Non-pulmonary Tuberculosis. As compared with the previous year, there was a decrease of 50 in the number of notifications of Diphtheria. The number of notifications of Scarlet Fever (13) represents a decrease ol36, as compared with that for 1933.
