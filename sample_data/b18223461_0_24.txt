22 INFANT MORTALITY. There were 15 deaths of infants under one year of age in Chelsea during the year. The infant mortality rate (deaths under 1 year per 1,000 live births) was 40 as compared with 33 in the. previous year. In London as a whole the rate of infant mortality was 50 per 1,000 live births.
