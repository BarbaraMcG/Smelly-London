42 Deptford has solderers and braziers at the rate of 8 per 1,000 and tinsmiths 12 per 1,000. Both in metal working as a whole and for the chief single occupations included, that of pressworking, stamping and drawing, Bermondsey, 66, and Deptford, 61, far exceed all other boroughs in the county. Deptford returns a high proportion for both sexes. Tin-box making is largely located in Deptford and Bermondsey.
