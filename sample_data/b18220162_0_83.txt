(a) Sale of Milk. In December, 1923, the attention of the Public Health Committee was drawn to the milk shops. To begin with, it was explained that in the City of London very considerable alterations of the Register had resulted from the survey of milksellers in consequence of the 1922 Milk Amendment Act.
