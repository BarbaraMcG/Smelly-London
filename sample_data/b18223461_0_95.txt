SANITARY CIRCUMSTANCES & ADMINISTRATION. During the year 8,344 inspections (including rc-inspections) were carried out by the Sanitary Inspectors. Included in this total number were 892 inspections on complaint of nuisance and 146 with reference to infectious disease. Legal proceedings under the Public Health (London) Act, 1936, in respect of sanitary conditions were undertaken in 21 instances. The number of inspections of Factories, Workshops and Workplaces was 133. Defects were found end remedied in 21 instances. 442 Intimation notices, 155 Statutory notices under the Public Health (London) Act, 1936, and 8 Statutory notices under byelaws for provision of dustbins were served during the year. DISINFECTION. During the year 217 rooms were disinfected after cases of infectious or other disease. Of these, 48 were rooms which had been occupied by tuberculous persons. In addition, 315 rooms were disinfected for the presence of vermin and 101 rooms for miscellaneous disinfection. At the Disin - fecting Station, 11,724 articles of bedding, etc., were disinfected and 3)397 articles destroyed in the incinerator, 442 rooms in connection with Civil Defence were also disin - fected.
